## World: Hardware Store Theme

### Room 1: Entrance (入口/iriguchi)
This is the main entrance of the hardware store, filled with promotional items and brochures.
- Vocabulary:
  - 入口 (iriguchi) - entrance
  - ブロシュア (buroshua) - brochure
  - カート (ka-to) - cart
  - 店員 (tenin) - store clerk

### Room 2: Tools Section (工具セクション/kougu sekushon)
This area is filled with all kinds of tools needed for home repairs and construction.
- Vocabulary:
  - ハンマー (hanma-) - hammer
  - ドライバー (doraiba-) - screwdriver
  - レンチ (renchi) - wrench
  - ニッパー (nip pa-) - pliers

### Room 3: Paint Section (塗料セクション/toryou sekushon)
This area contains a variety of paints, brushes and other painting supplies.
- Vocabulary:
  - 塗料 (toryou) - paint
  - ブラシ (burashi) - brush
  - ローラー (ro-ra-) - roller
  - パレット (paretto) - palette

### Room 4: Electrical Section (電気製品セクション/denki seihin sekushon)
This area contains electrical items such as wires, plugs, and light bulbs.
- Vocabulary:
  - 電球 (denkyuu) - light bulb
  - プラグ (puragu) - plug
  - 配線 (haisen) - wiring
  - 電池 (denchi) - battery

### Room 5: Plumbing Section (配管セクション/haikan sekushon)
This area contains various plumbing materials including pipes, faucets, and wrenches.
- Vocabulary:
  - パイプ (paipu) - pipe
  - 蛇口 (jaguchi) - faucet
  - プランジャー (puranja-) - plunger
  - テープ (te-pu) - tape

### Room 6: Checkout Counter (レジカウンター/reji kaunta-)
This is where customers pay for their purchases and ask for assistance.
- Vocabulary:
  - レジ (reji) - cash register
  - 会計 (kaikei) - payment
  - レシート (reshi-to) - receipt
  - 袋 (fukuro) - bag

### Room Connections:
- Entrance connects to Tools Section and Paint Section
- Tools Section connects to Electrical Section and Plumbing Section
- Paint Section connects to Electrical Section
- Electrical Section connects to Checkout Counter
- Plumbing Section connects to Checkout Counter

### NPCs and Conversation Vocabulary

1. Yamada-san - Store Clerk
   Location: Entrance
   Vocabulary:
   - お客様 (okyakusama) - customer
   - 推奨商品 (suisou shouhin) - recommended product
   - 営業時間 (eigyou jikan) - business hours

2. Suzuki-san - Tools Specialist
   Location: Tools Section
   Vocabulary:
   - 品質 (hinshitsu) - quality
   - 耐久性 (taikyusei) - durability
   - 使用方法 (shiyouhouhou) - how to use

3. Tanaka-san - Paint Expert
   Location: Paint Section
   Vocabulary:
   - 色合い (iroai) - color tone
   - 乾燥時間 (kansou jikan) - drying time
   - 塗り方 (nurikata) - how to paint

4. Satou-san - Cashier
   Location: Checkout Counter
   Vocabulary:
   - 割引 (waribiki) - discount
   - お釣り (otsuri) - change
   - ポイントカード (pointo ka-do) - point card

### Example Interactions:
1. "ハンマーを見つけます" (I find a hammer) at Tools Section
2. "ブラシを持ちます" (I hold a brush) at Paint Section
3. "電球を交換します" (I replace a light bulb) at Electrical Section
4. "パイプを測ります" (I measure a pipe) at Plumbing Section
5. "レジで会計します" (I pay at the cash register) at Checkout Counter
6. "店員に質問します" (I ask the store clerk) at Entrance

### NPC Interaction Examples:
1. "山田さんに推奨商品を尋ねます" (I ask Yamada-san about the recommended product) at Entrance
2. "鈴木さんにドライバーの使用方法を聞きます" (I ask Suzuki-san how to use the screwdriver) at Tools Section
3. "田中さんに塗り方を教えてもらいます" (I learn how to paint from Tanaka-san) at Paint Section
4. "佐藤さんにポイントカードを見せます" (I show the point card to Satou-san) at Checkout Counter
