## World: Cafe Theme

### Room 1: Entrance (エントランス/Entoransu)
A welcoming area where customers enter the cafe. 
- Vocabulary:
  - 扉 (tobira) - Door
  - 傘立て (kasatate) - Umbrella stand
  - メニューボード (menyūbōdo) - Menu board

### Room 2: Main Seating Area (メインシーティングエリア/Mein shītingu eria)
A comfortable and stylish space for customers to enjoy their orders.
- Vocabulary:
  - テーブル (tēburu) - Table
  - 椅子 (isu) - Chair
  - 窓 (mado) - Window

### Room 3: Counter (カウンター/Kauntā)
Where customers place their orders and baristas prepare beverages.
- Vocabulary:
  - レジスター (rejisutā) - Cash register
  - コーヒーマシン (kōhī mashin) - Coffee machine
  - ペストリーケース (pesutorī kēsu) - Pastry case

### Room 4: Kitchen (キッチン/Kitchin)
Where the cafe's food is prepared, not usually accessible to customers.
- Vocabulary:
  - レンジ (renji) - Microwave
  - 冷蔵庫 (reizōko) - Refrigerator
  - ストーブ (sutōbu) - Stove

### Room 5: Restrooms (トイレ/Toire)
A necessary facility for both staff and customers.
- Vocabulary:
  - トイレットペーパー (toiretto pēpā) - Toilet paper
  - 手洗い (tearai) - Sink
  - ハンドドライヤー (handodoraiyā) - Hand dryer

### Room 6: Patio (パティオ/Patio)
An outdoor seating area for customers to enjoy their beverages and meals.
- Vocabulary:
  - パラソル (parasoru) - Parasol
  - ベンチ (benchi) - Bench
  - プランター (purantā) - Planter

### Room Connections:
- Entrance connects to Main Seating Area and Counter
- Main Seating Area connects to Entrance and Patio
- Counter connects to Entrance and Kitchen
- Kitchen connects to Counter
- Restrooms connects to Main Seating Area
- Patio connects to Main Seating Area

### NPCs and Conversation Vocabulary

1. Yumi - Barista
   Location: Counter
   Vocabulary:
   - 注文 (chūmon) - Order
   - ドリップ (dorippu) - Drip (method of coffee brewing)
   - エスプレッソ (esupuresso) - Espresso

2. Hiroshi - Cook
   Location: Kitchen
   Vocabulary:
   - 材料 (zairyō) - Ingredients
   - 仕込み (shikomi) - Prep work
   - 料理 (ryōri) - Cooking

3. Keiko - Waitress
   Location: Main Seating Area
   Vocabulary:
   - 空く (aku) - To become available
   - クリーニング (kurīningu) - Cleaning
   - サービス (sābisu) - Service

4. Takeshi - Regular Customer
   Location: Patio
   Vocabulary:
   - おすすめ (osusume) - Recommendation
   - 予約 (yoyaku) - Reservation
   - 定番 (teiban) - Standard menu item

### Example Interactions:
1. "メニューボードを見る" (Look at the menu board) at Entrance
2. "テーブルに座る" (Sit at the table) at Main Seating Area
3. "コーヒーマシンを使う" (Use the coffee machine) at Counter
4. "冷蔵庫を開ける" (Open the refrigerator) at Kitchen
5. "トイレットペーパーを取る" (Take toilet paper) at Restrooms
6. "パラソルを開く" (Open the parasol) at Patio

### NPC Interaction Examples:
1. "ユミに注文をする" (Place an order with Yumi) at Counter
2. "ヒロシに材料を尋ねる" (Ask Hiroshi about the ingredients) at Kitchen
3. "ケイコにテーブルのクリーニングを頼む" (Ask Keiko to clean the table) at Main Seating Area
4. "タケシにおすすめを聞く" (Ask Takeshi for a recommendation) at Patio
