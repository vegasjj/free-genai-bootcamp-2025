## World: Hardware Store

### Room 1: Entrance (入口 / Iriguchi)
A welcoming entrance with sliding glass doors and a few promotional displays.
- Vocabulary:
  - 入口 (Iriguchi) - Entrance
  - ドア (Doa) - Door
  - ディスプレイ (Disupurei) - Display
  - ポスター (Posutā) - Poster

### Room 2: Tool Section (工具コーナー / Kōgu kōnā)
A section dedicated to various hand and power tools.
- Vocabulary:
  - 工具 (Kōgu) - Tools
  - ハンマー (Hanmā) - Hammer
  - ドライバー (Doraibā) - Screwdriver
  - 電動ドリル (Dendō doriru) - Power drill

### Room 3: Paint Section (ペンキコーナー / Penki kōnā)
A colorful section with an array of paints and brushes.
- Vocabulary:
  - ペンキ (Penki) - Paint
  - ブラシ (Burashi) - Brush
  - ローラー (Rōrā) - Roller
  - 色見本 (Iromihon) - Color swatch

### Room 4: Electrical Section (電気製品コーナー / Denki seihin kōnā)
A section with electrical items like cables, light bulbs, and extension cords.
- Vocabulary:
  - 電球 (Denkyū) - Light bulb
  - 電気コード (Denki kōdo) - Electrical cord
  - 電源タップ (Dengen tappu) - Power strip
  - 電池 (Denchi) - Battery

### Room 5: Plumbing Section (配管部品コーナー / Haikan buhin kōnā)
A section with plumbing parts like pipes, faucets, and fittings.
- Vocabulary:
  - 配管 (Haikan) - Plumbing
  - パイプ (Paipu) - Pipe
  - 蛇口 (Jaguchi) - Faucet
  - フィッティング (Fittingu) - Fitting

### Room 6: Checkout (レジ / Reji)
The checkout counter where customers pay for their purchases.
- Vocabulary:
  - レジ (Reji) - Cash register
  - お金 (Okane) - Money
  - レシート (Reshīto) - Receipt
  - 買い物袋 (Kaimono bukuro) - Shopping bag

### Room Connections:
- Entrance connects to Tool Section and Checkout
- Tool Section connects to Paint Section and Electrical Section
- Paint Section connects to Electrical Section
- Electrical Section connects to Plumbing Section
- Plumbing Section connects to Checkout
- Checkout connects to Entrance

### NPCs and Conversation Vocabulary:

1. Yamada-san - Store Manager
   Location: Entrance
   Vocabulary:
   - 売上 (Uriage) - Sales
   - 営業時間 (Eigyō jikan) - Business hours
   - 販促 (Hansoku) - Promotion

2. Suzuki-san - Tool Section Supervisor
   Location: Tool Section
   Vocabulary:
   - 手頃な価格 (Tegoro na kakaku) - Affordable price
   - 品質 (Hinshitsu) - Quality
   - 保証 (Hoshō) - Warranty

3. Tanaka-san - Paint Section Supervisor
   Location: Paint Section
   Vocabulary:
   - 色合い (Iroai) - Hue
   - 乾燥時間 (Kansō jikan) - Drying time
   - 一缶 (Ippō) - One can

4. Watanabe-san - Checkout Clerk
   Location: Checkout
   Vocabulary:
   - 会計 (Kaikei) - Checkout
   - 支払い方法 (Shiharai hōhō) - Payment method
   - ポイントカード (Pointo kādo) - Point card

### Example Interactions:
1. "ドアを開けます。" (I open the door.) at Entrance
2. "ハンマーを持ちます。" (I pick up the hammer.) at Tool Section
3. "ペンキを見ます。" (I look at the paint.) at Paint Section
4. "電球を変えます。" (I change the light bulb.) at Electrical Section
5. "パイプを測ります。" (I measure the pipe.) at Plumbing Section
6. "お金を数えます。" (I count the money.) at Checkout

### NPC Interaction Examples:
1. "山田さん、営業時間を教えてください。" (Yamada-san, please tell me the business hours.) with Yamada-san
2. "鈴木さん、このハンマーの保証はありますか。" (Suzuki-san, is there a warranty for this hammer?) with Suzuki-san
3. "田中さん、このペンキは何時間で乾きますか。" (Tanaka-san, how long does this paint take to dry?) with Tanaka-san
4. "渡辺さん、ポイントカードはありますか。" (Watanabe-san, do you have a point card?) with Watanabe-san
