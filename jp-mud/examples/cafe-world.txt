## Example World: Cafe Theme

### Room 1: Entrance (入口/iriguchi)
A welcoming entrance to the cafe with a sign and menu board.
- Vocabulary:
  - ドア (doa) - door
  - メニュー (menyuu) - menu
  - 看板 (kanban) - sign
  - 傘 (kasa) - umbrella

### Room 2: Counter Area (カウンター/kauntaa)
Where customers order and pay for their drinks.
- Vocabulary:
  - お金 (okane) - money
  - レシート (reshiito) - receipt
  - 財布 (saifu) - wallet
  - レジ (reji) - cash register

### Room 3: Seating Area (座席/zaseki)
The main cafe space with tables and chairs.
- Vocabulary:
  - 椅子 (isu) - chair
  - テーブル (teeburu) - table
  - 窓 (mado) - window
  - エアコン (eakon) - air conditioner

### Room 4: Coffee Bar (コーヒーバー/koohiibaa)
Where baristas prepare drinks.
- Vocabulary:
  - コーヒー (koohii) - coffee
  - カップ (kappu) - cup
  - お水 (omizu) - water

### Room 5: Kitchen (キッチン/kicchin)
Where food is prepared.
- Vocabulary:
  - ケーキ (keeki) - cake
  - お皿 (osara) - plate
  - フォーク (fooku) - fork
  - ナイフ (naifu) - knife

### Room 6: Rest Area (休憩室/kyuukeishitsu)
A relaxation space with magazines and wifi.
- Vocabulary:
  - 雑誌 (zasshi) - magazine
  - スマホ (sumaho) - smartphone
  - ソファー (sofaa) - sofa
  - 時計 (tokei) - clock

### Room Connections:
- Entrance connects to Counter Area and Seating Area
- Counter Area connects to Coffee Bar and Kitchen
- Seating Area connects to Rest Area
- Coffee Bar connects to Kitchen
- Kitchen connects to Rest Area

### NPCs and Conversation Vocabulary

1. 田中さん (Tanaka-san) - Cafe Manager
   Location: Counter Area
   Vocabulary:
   - 予約 (yoyaku) - reservation
   - サービス (saabisu) - service

2. 鈴木さん (Suzuki-san) - Barista
   Location: Coffee Bar
   Vocabulary:
   - 混雑 (konzatsu) - crowded
   - 特製 (tokusei) - special made

3. 佐藤さん (Satou-san) - Chef
   Location: Kitchen
   Vocabulary:
   - 手作り (tedzukuri) - handmade
   - 甘い (amai) - sweet

4. 山本さん (Yamamoto-san) - Server
   Location: Seating Area
   Vocabulary:
   - おすすめ (osusume) - recommendation
   - 美味しい (oishii) - delicious

### Example Interactions:
1. "ドアを開けます" (Open the door) at the entrance
2. "お金を使います" (Use money) at the counter
3. "椅子を見ます" (Look at the chair) in seating area
4. "コーヒーを飲みます" (Drink coffee) at coffee bar
5. "ケーキを食べます" (Eat cake) from kitchen
6. "雑誌を取ります" (Take magazine) in rest area

### NPC Interaction Examples:
1. "田中さんと話します" (Talk with Tanaka-san) - Learn about reservations
2. "鈴木さんと話します" (Talk with Suzuki-san) - Learn about special coffee
3. "佐藤さんと話します" (Talk with Satou-san) - Learn about handmade sweets
4. "山本さんと話します" (Talk with Yamamoto-san) - Get menu recommendations
